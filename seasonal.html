SEASONAL CLOTHING

Currently, fashion trends in the world are changing rapidly. However, depending on the season, fashion has the following characteristics:

Spring and autumn clothes Spring is the season when all things proliferate and the weather is warm and pleasant. Autumn is also a season when the climate is cool and comfortable. In these two seasons, you should choose clothes that are sewn with soft, light, loose materials, with many patterns and fun textures to always feel happy and comfortable.

Summer clothes: In the summer, the weather is hot and the temperature is high, making people more irritable and frustrated than usual. You should choose clothes with cool, cool colors to create a pleasant feeling for those around you. In addition, you should also choose clothes that are sewn with soft, cool, sweat-absorbent fabrics to always feel comfortable and comfortable. Simple, sophisticated with Vietnamese fashion brand

Winter clothes In the cold winter, it is advisable to choose clothes made of thick, light fabrics, preferably natural fur coats that keep the body warm, aesthetic and not too heavy, no one wants to wear. a few extra kilos on your body right?
